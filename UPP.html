User Privacy Notice

What is personal information?

Personal information is anything that could be used, either directly, or indirectly, to identify you — your name and address, date of birth, or credit card details for example.

What information does Salehero collect?

When you register with us, we collect your name and contact details. When you buy something, we ask for your payment details and delivery address.

If you're a seller, we ask you to provide a payment method which you'll use to cover your Salehero fees, and how you'd like to receive payment for the items you sell.

We also collect some information automatically such as cookies, web beacons, and what device you're using to view our site. If you'd like to know more about the types of data we collect automatically, please read our Cookie policy.

How does Salehero use my personal information?

We use your personal information to keep you safe on our site and protect you from fraud. We also use it to personalise your experience on Salehero by suggesting items we think you'd like based on what you've bought before, for example.

If you download our app, we note what device you're using so we can provide you with the relevant support for that device.

Who does Salehero share my personal information with?

We'll share your personal information with other Salehero members only to facilitate transactions and to allow them to contact you when you've completed a transaction together.

To help protect all of our members, we may also share your data with agencies that help us detect, prevent or investigate illegal acts or transactions on the site.

You'll find full details of who we may share your information with in the Disclosure section of the full Privacy Notice below.

Want to know more?

You can find our full Privacy Notice below. If you need any more information, or if you have a question or complaint, you'll find our contact details on our Global Privacy Centre, or you can write to our Global Privacy Office at Salehero.
